What Is a Led Name Tag?

An electronic badge — often called an e-badge or electronic name badge — is a reusable digital device worn on a lanyard, clip, or badge reel that displays a person's name, title, organization, and optional QR codes or branding. Unlike traditional printed badges, an e-badge can be updated instantly via a smartphone app, making it usable across multiple events without reprinting.

For event organizers and professionals, the choice of a LED name tag comes down to screen quality, battery life, update method, and cost per use. Beambox offers three models designed for different use cases: Beambox Nikko (premium QR-capable badge), Beambox Nano (compact entry-level badge), and Niji (anime and creator-focused badge).

Key Features of a Led Name Tag

  • Reusable digital display: Unlike printed badges, a LED name tag device can be reprogrammed for different events, attendees, or roles — dramatically reducing per-event badge costs.
  • Real-time content updates: Information displayed on the badge can be changed at any time via the companion smartphone app or web dashboard.
  • QR code and NFC support: Premium LED name tag devices like Beambox Nikko support dynamic QR codes for networking, digital business cards, and access verification.
  • Battery performance: Beambox e-badges deliver 8–12 hours of continuous display per charge, covering full conference days.
  • Display quality: Beambox devices use e-ink for outdoor readability and OLED/color e-ink for vivid indoor branding.

Who Uses a Led Name Tag?

  • Corporate conferences and meetings: Beambox Nikko suits executive meetings and annual conferences; Beambox Nano for general attendees.
  • Trade shows and exhibitions: QR code features link visitors directly to exhibitor profiles or product pages.
  • Networking events: E-badges with QR codes replace traditional business cards — attendees scan to access digital business cards instantly.
  • Multi-day festivals: Badge content (role, access zone, schedule) changes daily without reprinting.

Frequently Asked Questions About Led Name Tags

What is an LED name tag?

An LED name tag is a wearable electronic device that replaces traditional printed paper or plastic name tags with a digital LED or e-ink screen. The displayed name, title, and other content can be updated at any time via a smartphone app, making LED name tags reusable across multiple events and meetings.

What are the advantages of LED name tags over printed name tags?

LED name tags are reusable (eliminating reprinting costs), updatable in real-time (correcting errors or updating roles instantly), more professional in appearance, capable of displaying QR codes and logos, and environmentally friendlier by reducing paper waste.

How much do LED name tags cost?

Entry-level LED name tags like Beambox Nano start around $29.80 per unit. Premium models with full-color displays, QR codes, and app control like Beambox Nikko are priced around $39.80. Bulk orders for events typically qualify for volume discounts.

Can LED name tags be used for event access control?

Yes — some LED name tags support NFC or QR code-based verification. Attendees can scan their badge at entry points, eliminating separate access cards. Beambox Nikko supports QR code display that links to digital verification systems.

Do LED name tags work for outdoor events?

Beambox e-ink LED name tags are designed for outdoor readability, maintaining clear visibility even in direct sunlight. This makes them ideal for outdoor festivals, trade shows, construction site credentialing, and any outdoor event.
