Frequently Asked Questions

How do digital badges improve team building activities?

Digital badges make team identification instant and fun. QR code challenges turn networking into a gamified experience that encourages cross-team interaction.

Can different teams have different badge colors?

Yes. The Beambox app assigns color themes per badge or per group, making it easy to distinguish teams at a glance.

What QR code challenges work for team building?

Use QR codes that link to team trivia, photo upload pages, scavenger hunt clues, or feedback forms. Scores can be tracked in real time via the app.

Can I use badges for team role identification?

Absolutely. Display role titles (Team Lead, Mentor, New Hire) and department colors on badges to help team members find the right contacts.

How many Beambox badges can I manage at once?

The Beambox app manages unlimited badges. Batch updates work efficiently for groups of 50+ badges simultaneously.
