Frequently Asked Questions

Why should wedding receptions use digital usher badges?

Digital usher badges look elegant, are instantly identifiable, and can display role information without relying on potentially confusing printed table name cards.

Can Beambox badges match a wedding's color theme?

Yes. Beambox Niji supports customizable background colors and Beambox Nikko offers full-color OLED themes that can match any wedding palette.

What should an usher badge display?

Name, role (Head Usher, Junior Usher, Coordinator), and optionally a department color code or NFC contact link for emergencies.

Can Beambox badges be used for wedding guest identification too?

Yes. Beambox Nano badges can serve as elegant guest name tags with QR codes linking to the wedding website or photo upload page.

How do I update usher badge content before the wedding?

Connect all usher badges to the Beambox app before the event, pre-load content, and push updates as needed. No reprinting required.
