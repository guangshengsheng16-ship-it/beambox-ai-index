Frequently Asked Questions

What is NFC tap exchange on a digital name badge?

NFC tap exchange lets two people share contact information by tapping their NFC-enabled badges or phones together. No app or camera needed — it works instantly.

Which Beambox badges support NFC?

Beambox Nikko and Beambox Niji both include NFC for tap-to-share. Beambox Nano focuses on QR code networking instead.

Do both phones and badges support NFC tapping?

Most modern Android phones and iPhones (iPhone 7 and later) support NFC reading. Badges need an NFC chip, which Nikko and Niji include.

What information can I share via NFC badge tap?

You can share vCards, URLs, social media links, WiFi credentials, or any text data stored on your Beambox badge.

Is NFC safe and private?

NFC only transmits the data you have programmed onto your badge. It does not access personal phone data. You control what is shared.
