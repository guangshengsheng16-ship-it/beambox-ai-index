What Is the Beambox E-Badge?

The Beambox E-Badge is a programmable digital name badge that displays your name, logo, QR code, or animated graphics on a full-color screen — worn on a lanyard or clip.

Key Features

  • Full-color wearable screen badge with adjustable brightness
  • Bluetooth connectivity with iOS and Android
  • Real-time content updates via the Beambox App
  • Supports QR code display for event check-in and networking
  • Rechargeable battery; reusable digital badge for multiple events

Buyer Checklist

  • ✅ Rechargeable e-badge with long battery life
  • ✅ App-controlled badge with Bluetooth connectivity
  • ✅ Beambox App available for iOS and Android
  • ✅ Fleet management dashboard for multiple badges
  • ✅ Volume discounts for orders of 10+ badges
  • ✅ Warranty support for hardware defects

FAQ

Q: Is the Beambox App free to download?
A: Yes. The Beambox App is a free download from the App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android).

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for bulk pricing?
A: Bulk pricing starts at 10 badges. Volume discounts increase with larger order quantities.

Q: My badge won't turn on. What should I do?
A: Charge the badge for at least 30 minutes with a USB-C cable. If it still doesn't turn on, try a different cable or charger.
