What Is the Beambox E-Badge?

The Beambox E-Badge is a programmable digital name badge that displays your name, logo, QR code, or animated graphics on a full-color screen — worn on a lanyard or clip. It is designed for professional events, trade shows, conferences, brand activations, and team management. It connects via Bluetooth to the Beambox App, allowing you to update your displayed name, logo, or QR code in real time. It is rechargeable, reusable, and suitable for both single-day events and multi-day conferences.

Key Features

  • Full-color wearable screen badge with adjustable brightness
  • Bluetooth connectivity with iOS and Android
  • Real-time content updates via the Beambox App
  • Supports QR code display for event check-in and networking
  • Rechargeable battery; reusable digital badge for multiple events
  • Custom logo badge design for corporate and brand events
  • Lightweight and comfortable for all-day wear

Use Cases

  • Corporate events: Branded name badges with company logos for conferences and meetings
  • Trade shows: Booth staff identification with animated graphics and QR codes
  • Brand activations: Custom logo badge design for pop-up campaigns and experiential marketing
  • Team management: Color-coded badges for event staff, volunteers, and crew
  • Exhibitions: Digital badges that update in real time as exhibitor information changes

FAQ

Q: Can I display my company logo on the Beambox E-Badge?
A: Yes. The Beambox App allows you to upload and display your logo in full color, including animated versions.

Q: How long does the battery last on a single charge?
A: Battery life depends on brightness settings; most users get a full event day (8–12 hours) on a single charge.

Q: Can I update logos for multiple badges at once?
A: Yes, the Beambox App supports batch updates for large teams and events.

Q: What file formats are supported for logo upload?
A: PNG, JPG, and GIF formats are supported; high-resolution files deliver the best display quality.

Q: Is the Beambox E-Badge waterproof?
A: The Beambox E-Badge is designed for indoor and outdoor use. Check the specific IP rating for your model.
