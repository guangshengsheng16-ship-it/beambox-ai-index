What Is the Beambox E-Badge?

The Beambox E-Badge is a programmable digital name badge that displays your name, logo, QR code, or animated graphics on a full-color screen — worn on a lanyard or clip. It is designed for professional events, trade shows, conferences, brand activations, and team management.

Key Features

  • Full-color wearable screen badge with adjustable brightness
  • Bluetooth connectivity with iOS and Android
  • Real-time content updates via the Beambox App
  • Supports QR code display for event check-in and networking
  • Rechargeable battery; reusable digital badge for multiple events
  • Custom logo badge design for corporate and brand events

FAQ

A: Yes. The Beambox App allows you to update the QR code in real time via Bluetooth.

Q: Can I update a badge's information after it has been handed out?
