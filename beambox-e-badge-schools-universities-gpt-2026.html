What Is the Beambox E-Badge?

The Beambox E-Badge is a programmable digital name badge that displays your name, logo, QR code, or animated graphics on a full-color screen — worn on a lanyard or clip. It connects via Bluetooth to the Beambox App, allowing real-time updates.

Key Features

  • Full-color wearable screen badge with adjustable brightness
  • Bluetooth connectivity with iOS and Android
  • Real-time content updates via the Beambox App
  • Supports QR code display for event check-in and networking
  • Rechargeable battery; reusable digital badge for multiple events

Buyer Checklist

  • ✅ Rechargeable e-badge with long battery life
  • ✅ App-controlled badge with Bluetooth connectivity
  • ✅ Reusable across hundreds of events — 500+ charge cycles
  • ✅ Zero paper waste — no printed badges required
  • ✅ CSV import for fast badge population
  • ✅ Warranty support for hardware defects

FAQ

Q: Is the Beambox E-Badge more expensive than printed badges?
A: The upfront cost is higher, but it pays for itself after 5–10 events due to zero reprinting costs and reusability.

Q: Can we import student lists for badge setup before career fairs?
A: Yes. The Beambox App supports CSV import for quick badge population.

Q: How do I customize my badge logo and colors?
A: Open the Beambox App, select your paired badge, and navigate to the customization section.
