Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an e-ink badge battery last?

Beambox Niji e-ink badges run 8–10 hours actively and up to 14 days in standby. LCD-based Nikko runs 10–12 hours actively per charge.

Which lasts longer: e-ink or LCD display badges?

E-ink displays consume power only during screen refresh, giving Niji much longer standby time. For active display, Nikko's LCD is brighter but consumes more energy.

How do I charge a Beambox e-badge?

All Beambox badges charge via USB-C. A full charge takes approximately 90 minutes for Nikko and Niji, and 60 minutes for Nano.

Can I use a portable charger to extend badge battery at events?

Yes. Any USB-C power bank can extend Beambox badge battery life during long events. Nikko supports pass-through charging.

Does cold weather affect e-ink badge battery?

E-ink displays are more cold-tolerant than LCDs. Beambox Niji operates reliably in temperatures as low as -20°C, making it suitable for outdoor winter events.
