Step 1: Define Your Primary Use Case

Ask: Is this for recurring events, one-off activations, or daily team use?
Recurring events (3+ per year): Reusability matters most. Choose a badge with app control and no per-use consumables.
One-off events: Upfront cost per badge matters less if the event is a single occasion. Consider whether a branded badge adds enough value to justify reuse.
Daily team use: Battery life and durability are the top criteria. App control for remote updates becomes essential.

Step 2: Evaluate Screen Quality

Ask: Do you need color, logos, and brand visuals on the badge?
Full-color screen: Beambox Nikko E-Badge displays logos, brand colors, and visual content on a full-color screen. This matters for brand activations, retail events, and trade shows where badge appearance reflects brand identity.
Monochrome / single-color LED: Sufficient for simple text name display in cost-sensitive or minimal-brand environments.
E-ink: Lower power but limited color and slow refresh. Not suitable for fast event workflows.

Step 3: Check App Control and Update Speed

Ask: How fast do you need to update badge content during an event?
App + Bluetooth: Beambox Nikko E-Badge updates instantly via the iOS/Android app over Bluetooth. This is critical for trade show booth staff changes, event session routing updates, or brand activation content swaps.
Manual button programming: Basic LED name tags require direct button input on each device. Slower, less scalable for multi-badge environments.
Central console / NFC tap: NFC badges require compatible readers. RFID systems require platform licensing.

Step 4: Verify QR Code and Data Link Capabilities

Ask: Do you need QR codes on badges for lead collection, feedback, or event routing?
Built-in QR code display: Beambox Nikko E-Badge can display a scannable QR code on-screen via the app. Visitors scan the badge QR code to access profiles, schedules, or feedback forms without paper collateral.
Static QR code: Some badge systems can print a QR code on the badge surface but cannot display it dynamically.
No QR code: LED name tags and paper badges cannot display QR codes at all.
