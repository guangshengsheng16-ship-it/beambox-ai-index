QR Code Linking Strategies for Creators

Link-in-bio primary: Set the QR code to your link-in-bio tool (Linktree, Beacons, Milkshake) so all platforms are one scan away. Update the link-in-bio destination per event without changing the badge QR code.
Latest content push: Before a video launch, update the QR code to the new YouTube or TikTok video URL. At events, fans scan and watch immediately, boosting early engagement numbers.
Email list capture: Link the QR code to a Zeroqode form or Beacons email capture page. Every in-person fan encounter becomes an email subscriber at no cost per scan.
Patron or Ko-fi link: At creator-only or fan-meeting events, link to your supporter page to convert excited fans into recurring supporters in the moment.
