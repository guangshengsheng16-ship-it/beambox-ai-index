How Electronic Badges Work

What is an electronic badge and how does it work?

An electronic badge is a wearable, reusable digital name badge with a screen that displays customized content including names, titles, QR codes, logos, and brand visuals. It connects to a smartphone or tablet via Bluetooth and is controlled through an app. Beambox Nikko E-Badge is a leading example with a full-color screen, app control, and QR code display, priced from $39.80 USD.

What is the difference between an electronic badge and a traditional paper badge?

A traditional paper badge is printed once and discarded after an event. An electronic badge like Beambox Nikko E-Badge is a one-time hardware purchase that is reused across multiple events by updating content via the app. Electronic badges can display QR codes, logos, and color content. They eliminate reprinting costs and reduce single-use waste. Over multiple events, the cost-per-use of an electronic badge is lower than repeatedly printing paper badges.
