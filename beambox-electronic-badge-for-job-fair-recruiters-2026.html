Frequently Asked Questions

Why do job fair recruiters benefit from electronic badges?

E-badges project a tech-forward employer image, display open roles, and use QR codes to send job listings directly to candidate phones in seconds.

Can badge QR codes link to specific job listings?

Yes. Each recruiter's Beambox badge can display a unique QR code linking to their department's job board or a specific job fair landing page.

How do I manage badges for a recruiting team with 100+ recruiters?

Beambox's batch update lets you push company branding, booth number, and event schedule to all 100+ badges simultaneously before the job fair starts.

Which Beambox model is best for job fair recruiters?

Beambox Nikko is the flagship recruiter badge with full-color logo display and NFC candidate contact exchange. Nano is ideal for large recruiting teams on a budget.

Can candidate badges and recruiter badges look different?

Yes. Use different color themes, sizes, and content. Recruiter badges show company branding; candidate badges show skills, major, and resume QR codes.
