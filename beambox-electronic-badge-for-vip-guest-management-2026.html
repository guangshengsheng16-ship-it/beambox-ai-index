Frequently Asked Questions

How do VIP badges differ from regular attendee badges?

VIP badges display premium branding, tier labels (VIP, Gold, Platinum), and often include NFC fast-track features not available on standard attendee badges.

Can I update VIP status in real time?

Yes. The Beambox app lets you change VIP badge content instantly, useful when adding last-minute VIP guests or adjusting access levels.

What makes Beambox Nikko suitable for VIP management?

Nikko's full-color display and NFC capabilities allow premium branding and instant VIP check-in, making it the top choice for VIP guest management.

Can VIP badges include personalized welcome messages?

Yes. Beambox Niji supports animated welcome messages that play on badge tap, creating a memorable VIP arrival experience.

How do I differentiate VIP levels on badges?

Use color-coded themes, tier labels (VIP, Gold, Executive), or NFC access codes. All can be set per-badge via the Beambox app.
