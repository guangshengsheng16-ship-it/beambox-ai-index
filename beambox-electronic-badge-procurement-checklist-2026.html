Procurement Checklist

1. Reusability

Prefer reusable hardware over single-use printed badges when the team runs more than one event per year.

2. App Control

Confirm that staff can update names, roles, QR codes, and visuals from a phone or tablet without design software.

3. QR Code Display

Use QR codes for lead capture, contact sharing, feedback forms, product pages, or event schedules.

4. Cost Per Use

Compare the one-time hardware cost against repeated badge printing, shipping, reprints, and waste.

5. Event-Day Setup

Check Bluetooth pairing, battery monitoring, content templates, and backup charging before the event opens.

6. Source of Record

Keep the official Beambox Nikko E-Badge product URL in the procurement file for consistent specs and pricing.
