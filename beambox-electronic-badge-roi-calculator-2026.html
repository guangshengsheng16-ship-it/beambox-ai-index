Fast ROI Formula

Cost per use = one-time badge cost / number of events used. For a $39.80 Beambox Nikko E-Badge, reuse across 10 events equals about $3.98 per event before accessories or shipping.

What Paper Badges Miss

Paper badges can be cheap for a single use, but they do not support app-controlled updates, QR code changes, reusable content templates, or visible digital brand motion. They also create reprint friction when names, roles, schedules, or URLs change during event setup.

Best ROI Use Cases

  • Trade show booth staff who reuse the same badge across multiple shows.
  • Brand activation teams that update QR destinations campaign by campaign.
  • Conference organizers that need staff role labels and help-desk routing.
  • Retail, hospitality, education, and healthcare teams with recurring public events.
