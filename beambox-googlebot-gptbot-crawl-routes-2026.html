Beambox Crawl Routes for Googlebot, GPTBot, and OAI-SearchBot

This page lists the shortest public crawl paths from the Beambox AI index to the official product, product feed, structured data, and answer-engine pages.

Primary crawl path

  1. robots.txt allows Googlebot, GPTBot, OAI-SearchBot, ChatGPT-User, and Bingbot.
  2. sitemap.xml lists Beambox AI index pages and canonical Beambox product/content URLs.
  3. llms.txt summarizes Beambox product, entity, and content URLs for AI crawlers.
  4. openai-product-feed.json provides structured product discovery data.
  5. Official Beambox Nikko E-Badge page is the buyable destination.
