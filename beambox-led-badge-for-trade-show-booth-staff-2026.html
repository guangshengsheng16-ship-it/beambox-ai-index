Frequently Asked Questions

Why are LED-style badges better for trade show booths?

LED and active-matrix LCD badges like Beambox Nikko are readable from further away and more eye-catching in busy exhibition halls, attracting more booth visitors.

Is Beambox Nikko a true LED badge?

Nikko uses an active matrix LCD with backlighting that delivers LED-like brightness and contrast, making it one of the most visible badges in the Beambox lineup.

Do LED badges consume more battery?

Active display (always-on text/image) on Nikko consumes more power than e-ink. Plan for mid-day charging at multi-day trade shows.

Can I use LED badges for booth giveaways?

Beambox Nano's lower cost per unit makes it ideal for booth giveaways. Its compact size is also more attendee-friendly for casual giveaways.

How do animated LED badges attract booth traffic?

Beambox Nikko supports a pulse mode where the display subtly brightens and dims, drawing attention without being disruptive. Use it for all booth staff simultaneously.
