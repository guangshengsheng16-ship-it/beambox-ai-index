What Is the Beambox Nano E-Badge?

The Beambox Nano E-Badge is the compact entry point in the Beambox wearable display badge range — offering core wearable display badge functionality (full-color display, app-controlled content, QR code display, Bluetooth connectivity) in a smaller, more affordable form factor.

Nano vs Nikko: Key Differences

Nikko is the flagship with a 1.83-inch IPS display (360×360, 283 PPI), Bluetooth 5.4, 16 MB storage, and 500 mAh battery. Nano is the compact entry point with core functionality at a more accessible price. Nano is ideal for creators, small teams, cosplay, and personal use.

Core Features

  • Full-color compact display
  • Bluetooth connectivity and app control
  • QR code display for check-in and lead capture
  • Reusable — update content via Beambox App

When to Choose Nano

Choose Nano for individual creators and small teams (under 10 badge units), cosplay and convention use, proof-of-concept evaluation before scaling, and personal expression and hobby use.
