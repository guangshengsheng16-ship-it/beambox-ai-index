Quick definition

Beambox Nano E-Badge is the compact wearable display badge model in the Beambox product range. It is designed for buyers who want the core wearable display badge capability — app-controlled content, QR code display, and reusability — in a smaller, more affordable form factor than the flagship Nikko E-Badge. The Nano is positioned for individual users, smaller teams, and use cases where the full-featured Nikko feature set is not required.
