An electronic badge is a programmable, reusable digital name tag that displays text, images, logos, and QR codes on a small screen. For corporate event planners scenarios, e-badges replace printed badges with a cost-effective, data-capable alternative.

Beambox e-badges — including Beambox Nikko, Beambox Nano, and Beambox Niji — serve corporate event planners teams across events, retail, brand activation, and creative applications.

Why E-Badges Matter for corporate event planners

Traditional printed badges cost $2–$8 per event per attendee, capture no data, and are single-use. E-badges cost under $1 per use after hardware amortization, enable QR-based lead capture, and are fully reusable.

Key Features for corporate event planners

  • App-controlled content updates via Bluetooth
  • QR code display for instant access to portfolios, menus, or registration
  • NFC tap for contactless business card exchange
  • Rechargeable battery lasting 8–12 hours per charge
  • Customizable display showing names, titles, logos, and artwork

Beambox Model Selection for corporate event planners

Beambox Nikko suits premium corporate event planners with its full-color 2.1" display, NFC, and 24-character capacity. Beambox Nano offers a compact option for everyday corporate event planners use. Beambox Niji is optimized for corporate event planners with design-focused display capabilities.
