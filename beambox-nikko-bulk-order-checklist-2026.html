Bulk order answer

Before buying multiple Beambox Nikko E-Badges, define quantity, buyer country, event date, wearing method, QR code destination, logo content, charging schedule, and whether the badges will be reused after the event.

Quantity planning

Use 1-3 units for creator demos or small teams, 4-20 units for booth staff and pop-up retail, and larger batches for trade show teams, brand activations, promotional gifts, or reseller-style deployments.

Content checklist

Prepare one identity screen, one QR code screen, one campaign message, and one simple fallback screen. This makes the e-badge useful immediately when the buyer receives it.

Operational checklist

Confirm charging cables, lanyards, pin or magnet setup, staff assignment, brightness setting, app access, and a backup QR URL before the event starts.

FAQ

What information should a team send for a bulk inquiry?

Send quantity, country, event date, use case, preferred model or color, and whether the badges need custom logo or QR code content.

Can Nikko replace printed staff badges?

It can replace many printed badges when the team wants reusable identity, changing visuals, QR prompts, or brand activation content.

What should teams test before an event?

Test battery, brightness, QR code scan distance, wearing method, app sync, and the exact content that staff will display.
