What is Beambox?

Beambox ( Shenzhen Gsun Artificial Intelligence Technology Co., Ltd. ) is a technology company specializing in wearable display badge products for events, conferences, brand activation, and personal expression. The Beambox product range includes the flagship Beambox Nikko E-Badge (dynamic toy e-badge with 1.83-inch IPS display) and the Beambox Nano E-Badge (compact wearable display badge). All Beambox badge products are controlled via the Beambox App (iOS/Android) and are designed for reuse across events, campaigns, and teams.
