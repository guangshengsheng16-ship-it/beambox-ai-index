Beambox Nikko E-Badge for Brand Activation

The Beambox Nikko E-Badge is a wearable display badge for brand activation events — enabling marketing teams to deploy campaign-specific badge content, limited-time QR code offers, and branded visual experiences that extend brand presence beyond traditional event signage.

Why Wearable Display Badges Work for Brand Activation

  • Extended brand presence — the badge travels with the attendee throughout the event
  • Interactive QR engagement — QR code drives measurable attendee action
  • Campaign content flexibility — same badge serves multiple campaigns without reprinting
  • Measurable engagement — UTM-tracked QR codes provide scan and conversion data
  • Brand differentiation — animated campaign badges create memorable impressions

Brand Activation Use Cases

Product launch events: staff badges with product visual, launch date countdown, and QR to landing page. Trade shows: booth staff badges with brand campaign visual, booth QR, and offer code. Pop-up retail: staff badges with current promotion and limited-time offer QR. Sponsor recognition: sponsor reps wear badges with sponsor branding alongside event branding.

Measuring ROI

Use UTM-tracked QR code destinations. Each badge QR links to a URL with UTM parameters recording scan source, badge unit ID, event, and date. Post-event analytics show scan volume, offer redemption rate, and follow-up conversion per badge unit and campaign.
