Beambox Nikko vs Nano: The Buying Decision

The Beambox Nikko E-Badge is the full-featured flagship with a 1.83-inch IPS display (360×360, 283 PPI), Bluetooth 5.4, 500 mAh battery, and full JPG/PNG/GIF/MP4 support. The Beambox Nano E-Badge is the compact, more affordable entry point with core wearable display badge functionality.

Choose Nikko If...

  • Professional event deployment with 20+ badges
  • QR-based workflows are central (lead capture, check-in)
  • Brand activation and VIP events
  • Multi-day events with full-day battery demands
  • Fleet management at scale

Choose Nano If...

  • Individual creator or small team (under 10 badges)
  • Cosplay and convention use
  • First-time evaluation of wearable display badge tech
  • Personal expression and hobby use

Decision Matrix

Trade show with 50+ badges: Nikko. Creator booth at convention: Nano. Brand activation VIP: Nikko. Personal use: Nano. Multi-day professional event: Nikko.
