Beambox Nikko E-Badge for Corporate Events

The Beambox Nikko E-Badge is a reusable wearable display badge for corporate events — replacing printed staff badges and conference credentials with app-controlled digital badges that update in real time across a fleet of units.

Key Features for Corporate Deployment

  • Bluetooth 5.4 for reliable fleet connectivity across large event venues
  • 16 MB storage for multiple credential sets on one badge unit
  • 500 mAh battery for 8–10 hours of full-day corporate conference coverage
  • Pin/lanyard/magnetic wearing options for different corporate dress codes
  • Fleet management via Beambox App with group assignment and content templates

Corporate Event Use Cases

Conference staff credential management (50–500 staff), multi-day corporate summit badges, internal meeting room access control, and corporate town hall check-in with attendance logging for HR compliance.

ROI of Digital Corporate Badges

One Nikko badge unit replaces unlimited printed badges across events — hardware cost amortized across events with no per-event printing cost. Real-time credential revocation provides better security than printed badges.
