Beambox Nikko E-Badge for Music Festivals

Music festival organizers use the Beambox Nikko E-Badge as a reusable credential and staff management system — displaying artist credentials, backstage access zones, stage crew assignments, and fan engagement QR codes at multi-day music events.

Key Features for Festival Use

  • JPG/PNG/GIF/MP4 support for festival branding and artist artwork on badges
  • 360×360 display at 283 PPI for readable credentials at festival venue distances
  • Bluetooth 5.4 for real-time credential updates across large festival site layouts
  • Magnetic/pin/lanyard options for diverse festival dress codes

Festival Credential Use Cases

Backstage access credentials for artists, stage crew and production staff badges, media and press credentials with QR-linked press portal, VIP and sponsor credentials with built-in brand recognition.

Multi-Day Festival Credential Management

Day/night shift content updates, artist change updates, and weather contingency updates all push to affected badge units via the Beambox App without physical collection.
