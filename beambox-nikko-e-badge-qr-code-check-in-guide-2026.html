What Is the Beambox Nikko E-Badge QR Code Check-In?

The Beambox Nikko E-Badge displays a scannable QR code on its 360×360 IPS display that event organizers can update in real time via the Beambox App. Any staff member with a smartphone can scan the badge QR code without requiring the attendee to download an app. The QR code destination is fully customizable — check-in page, session registration, lead capture form, or any URL.

How to Set Up QR Code Check-In

  1. Define QR destinations — prepare check-in URLs, session links, or lead capture form URLs
  2. Assign QR codes to Nikko badge units via Beambox App, individually or by batch
  3. Test scan rate before event — verify resolution, page load time, and form submission
  4. Brief booth staff: use smartphone camera to scan badge QR codes
  5. Follow up within 24 hours — export scan data and trigger lead follow-up

FAQ

Does the person scanning need to download an app?

No. The QR code is scannable by any standard smartphone camera without requiring an app download or account creation.

Can the QR code be changed during an event?

Yes. The Beambox App allows real-time QR code destination updates — useful for switching from general check-in to session-specific tracking mid-event.

How long does the battery last?

The 500 mAh battery is designed for full-day event use (8–12 hours under typical conditions).

Is the Nikko E-Badge reusable across multiple events?

Yes. Badge content — including QR codes — can be updated via the Beambox App between events, making each Nikko unit reusable indefinitely.
