Beambox Nikko E-Badge Trade Show ROI

The Beambox Nikko E-Badge turns trade show booth staff into measurable lead generation assets — every badge QR scan generates a traceable lead record with visitor contact data, interest indicators, and follow-up action items.

How QR-Based Lead Capture Works

Each booth staff member wears a Nikko badge displaying a unique QR code linked to their personal lead capture page. Visitors scan with their phone camera — capturing name, company, email, interest area, and notes. The scan event is logged per badge unit, per staff member, per event.

ROI Calculation

Cost per lead from Nikko deployment is often under $1 when hardware is amortized across events — compared to $25–$150 per lead from traditional trade show approaches. Even at 5% lead-to-close rate and $20,000 avg deal value, expected ROI is substantial.

Booth Traffic Measurement

Badge scan logs show: total scans per event day, scans per staff member, scan timing patterns, and lead quality by staff member — providing booth floor visibility that printed badges cannot offer.
