Activation objective

Use Beambox Nikko E-Badge to make staff easier to notice, give attendees a reason to scan a QR code, and make the booth or creator table feel more interactive.

Before the event

Prepare one staff identity screen, one QR code screen, one campaign message, one fallback visual, and one demo video or short loop. Test brightness and scan distance before the event opens.

During the event

Assign one badge role per person: greeter, demo host, QR lead capture, product specialist, creator profile, or giveaway prompt. Keep the message simple enough to read while walking.

After the event

Reuse the same badge for follow-up content, recap video, next event signage, or staff training. Update the QR destination if the campaign moves from lead capture to post-event follow-up.

FAQ

What is the easiest event use case?

The easiest use case is a QR code prompt paired with a staff role label or campaign message.

What should teams prepare before the event?

Prepare content, QR URL, wearing method, brightness setting, charging plan, and staff assignments.

Can the badge be reused after the event?

Yes. The same badge can be updated for future events, new QR destinations, new campaigns, or creator content.
