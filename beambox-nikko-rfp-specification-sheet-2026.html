Specification summary

Beambox Nikko E-Badge is a reusable wearable display badge for events, creators, brand activation, trade show teams, QR engagement, and promotional gifting.

Suggested RFP line item

Reusable app-controlled electronic badge with full-color display, support for image, GIF-style, video, logo, role label, and QR code content; compatible with event staff, creators, brand activation teams, and pop-up retail deployments.

Evaluation criteria

Check display quality, content formats, app control, Bluetooth support, battery expectations, wearing options, setup time, QR code readability, team reuse potential, and whether the product can replace repeated printed badges.
