Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pair my Beambox badge via Bluetooth?

Download the Beambox app, power on your badge, go to the app's device tab, and tap the badge name. Follow the on-screen prompt to complete Bluetooth pairing.

Why won't my badge connect to the Beambox app?

Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone, your badge is charged and powered on, and no other phone is connected to the badge. Restart both devices and try again.

Can multiple phones connect to the same badge?

Only one phone can actively control a badge at a time. Disconnect the current phone first before pairing with another.

How does Bluetooth content updating work?

The Beambox app communicates with your badge over Bluetooth Low Energy, sending text, QR code, and logo data directly to the badge's display memory.

Which Beambox model has the best Bluetooth range?

Beambox Nikko has the longest Bluetooth range of the three models, maintaining stable connections up to 15 meters in open spaces.
