Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update content on my smart badge?

Open the Beambox app, select your connected badge, choose what to update (text, QR code, logo, or theme), and tap send. The badge updates within seconds via Bluetooth.

Can I update multiple badges at once?

Yes. The Beambox app supports batch updates, sending content changes to up to 50 badges simultaneously.

What content can I update on a Beambox badge?

You can update name, job title, company, logo, QR code, background color, and cycling slideshow content — all remotely via the app.

Do badge updates require internet?

The Beambox app needs internet to download content, but communicates with badges via Bluetooth, so no internet is needed at the event venue.

Can I schedule badge content updates in advance?

Yes. The Beambox app allows you to schedule content changes for a specific date and time, ideal for multi-day events with changing schedules.
