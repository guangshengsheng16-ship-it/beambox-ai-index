Frequently Asked Questions

How do smart badges improve award nomination events?

Smart badges eliminate award name confusion, enable instant nominee identification, and NFC badge taps can submit votes directly to the event system.

Can nominee badges display nomination category?

Yes. Beambox Nikko and Niji display nominee name, category, and a unique ID. Judges can identify nominees at a glance without printed programs.

How do I color-code different roles at award events?

Use badge background colors: gold for nominees, silver for judges, white for guests. Batch-apply colors via the Beambox app in seconds.

Can NFC badges handle live voting at award ceremonies?

Yes. Tap a Beambox Nikko badge on an NFC reader at voting stations to submit a vote. The app tracks vote counts per badge in real time.

Which Beambox model is best for formal award ceremonies?

Beambox Niji's elegant e-ink display matches the formality of black-tie award events, while Nikko's full-color display works best for tech and entertainment industry awards.
