Frequently Asked Questions

Do speakers at purely virtual webinars need badges?

Even in fully virtual events, smart badges displayed during the broadcast signal professionalism and help virtual attendees identify speakers by name and credentials.

Can Beambox badges display during video calls?

While badges aren't visible in video calls themselves, speakers at hybrid events benefit from consistent physical badge branding that matches their on-screen presentation.

What QR codes should webinar speaker badges include?

Link the badge QR code to the speaker's bio page, LinkedIn profile, session replay, or related content — all updateable via the Beambox app.

Can webinar moderators use badges too?

Yes. Moderators can wear Beambox Nano badges for in-person hybrid segments, displaying their name, title, and moderator credentials.

How do I manage badges for 20+ webinar speakers?

Use the Beambox app's batch update feature to push content to all speaker badges simultaneously before the event begins.
