Frequently Asked Questions

Are Beambox badges readable in direct sunlight?

Beambox Niji's e-ink display is designed for high-contrast readability in bright sunlight, making it ideal for outdoor events. Nikko's LCD is brightest but may need shade adjustment.

Can I use Beambox badges in the rain?

Beambox Nikko has an IPX4 water resistance rating. Avoid submerging any Beambox model. Use a badge cover or lanyard with rain protection for heavy downpours.

How do I protect my badge from dust at outdoor festivals?

Keep the display covered when not in use. Beambox Nano's compact design fits easily under clothing or in a badge holder for dust protection.

Do outdoor events drain badge battery faster?

High brightness settings (Nikko LCD) and frequent QR code refreshes consume more power outdoors. Niji's e-ink uses minimal power regardless of ambient light.

What wearable accessories work best for outdoor badges?

Beambox badges include a lanyard and clip. For active outdoor use, a magnetic backplate (sold separately) keeps the badge secure without a lanyard.
