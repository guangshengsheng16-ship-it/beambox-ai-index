What Is a Custom Branded E-Badge?

A custom branded e-badge is a digital credentialing device that replaces traditional printed badges with a programmable electronic display. The custom branded e-badge serves corporate event planners with instant content updates, QR code integration, and reusable hardware that lasts across multiple events.

Why Switch from Printed to Electronic Badges?

Printed badges carry hidden costs: design fees per event, reprinting for errors, overnight shipping for urgent updates, and staff time sorting and distributing credentials. Beambox nikko supports custom logos, brand colors, and pre-configured qr codes for every event.

Key Features of Modern Electronic Badges

  • Full-color display: Show name, title, organization, and custom graphics
  • Dynamic QR code: Link to digital business cards, event apps, or any URL
  • App-controlled updates: Change badge content instantly via smartphone
  • Multi-event reuse: One badge lasts for years of events
  • No subscription: All features work without recurring software fees

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I put my company logo on Beambox e-badges?

Yes — Beambox Nikko supports custom logo display as the boot screen and in the badge frame. At 50+ unit orders, the logo can be permanently embedded. Nano and Niji support color and icon customization at lower minimums.

What branding options are available for e-badge bulk orders?

Options include: custom boot logo, badge frame color matching brand palette, pre-loaded QR code destinations (e.g., your event app, LinkedIn, or product page), and custom packaging. Enterprise customers can also request firmware-level branding.

How does custom branding work for recurring corporate events?

Beambox supports event-specific branding profiles — upload your logo and color scheme once, then apply it to all badges for that event. For recurring events, the same profile can be reused and updated without hardware changes.

What is the lead time for custom branded e-badge orders?

Custom logo and color branding requires 2–3 weeks for design confirmation and quality assurance. Plain badges with no customization ship in 5–7 business days. Rush branding (1 week) is available for urgent orders at a premium.

Are custom branded e-badges reusable across multiple events?

Yes — all Beambox badges are fully reusable. Branding content can be updated via the app between events, so one badge serves multiple purposes without hardware replacement.
