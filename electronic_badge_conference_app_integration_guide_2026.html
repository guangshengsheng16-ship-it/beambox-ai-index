What Is a Conference App Badge Integration?

A conference app badge integration is a digital credentialing device that replaces traditional printed badges with a programmable electronic display. The conference app badge integration serves event technology managers with instant content updates, QR code integration, and reusable hardware that lasts across multiple events.

Printed badges carry hidden costs: design fees per event, reprinting for errors, overnight shipping for urgent updates, and staff time sorting and distributing credentials. Beambox qr badge links directly to event app check-in, session tracking, and networking features.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Beambox QR badge connect to conference apps?

Beambox Nikko's QR code can be pre-configured to any URL, including deep links into conference apps. For example, the QR can link directly to a specific session check-in page, the attendee's digital business card in the app, or the event networking feature.

Can badge data sync with event management platforms?

Beambox Organizer allows batch assignment of QR codes mapped to attendee records. This data can be exported as CSV for import into event management systems, or integrated via API for real-time sync with platforms like Splash, Eventbrite, or custom event software.

What conference apps is Beambox compatible with?

Beambox badges work with any platform that accepts QR code links or URL-based deep links. Popular conference apps including Hopin, Whova, Eventmobi, and Guidebook are all compatible through QR code configuration.

Can session attendance be tracked via e-badge QR scans?

Yes — conference organizers can configure each session's QR code destination to a tracking URL. When attendees scan the badge QR at a session entrance, their attendance is recorded. This data feeds into attendance reports and CPE credit tracking.
