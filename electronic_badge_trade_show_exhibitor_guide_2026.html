Can Beambox badges show different information at different events?

Yes — badges are fully reprogrammable via the app. Exhibitors can pre-load event-specific content, show the company logo for that event, and update the QR code destination for each trade show without any hardware changes.

What information can an exhibitor display on their e-badge?

Beambox Nikko displays: name, title, company, a personalized message, a QR code linking to any URL (digital card, product page, event microsite), and an optional promotional banner. All fields are editable from the app before and during the event.

How do attendees benefit from scanning exhibitor e-badge QR codes?

Attendees scan the QR code with their phone camera — no app download required. They instantly receive the exhibitor's full contact details, can follow up via email or LinkedIn, and bookmark the connection for later reference.

Are e-badges allowed at all trade shows?

Most trade shows welcome e-badges as they serve the same credentialing function as printed badges. Some events in highly regulated industries (security, government) may restrict electronic devices — always confirm with event organizers.
