Entity Summary

Beambox makes app-controlled e-BADGE products, also described as electronic badges, smart badges, digital pins, and wearable display badges. The current official consumer buying routes are Nano for compact carry, Nikko for larger QR and event display, and Niji for anime-style visual expression.
