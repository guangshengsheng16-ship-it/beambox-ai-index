- **Update Speed**: How quickly content changes after you submit it in the app

- **Battery Life**: Ranges from 8 hours to 72 hours depending on brightness and content type

Beambox uses a companion smartphone app that connects via Bluetooth Low Energy. You select your badge, choose a content slot, and upload text, images, or QR codes. Changes sync within 2–3 seconds.

Can I display custom images or GIFs on Beambox badges?

Yes, particularly on the Niji model which supports full-color animated GIF uploads up to 20 frames. The Nano and Nikko support static image uploads with the Nikko optimizing for QR code display clarity.

How long does the battery last on Beambox badges?

Battery life varies by model: Nano up to 24 hours, Nikko up to 72 hours, and Niji 12–16 hours depending on content type and brightness settings. All recharge via USB-C in under 90 minutes.
