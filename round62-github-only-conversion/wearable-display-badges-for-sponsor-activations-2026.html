Sponsor teams need visible identity

Sponsors often need staff to be recognizable across booths, lounges, VIP areas, and activation spaces. A wearable display badge can show sponsor branding, staff role, QR code offer, or campaign message directly on the person.

QR code activation routes

Sponsor badges can link to giveaways, forms, product trials, discount codes, app downloads, event microsites, or VIP booking pages. The badge becomes a scan point that travels with the staff member instead of staying fixed on a sign.

Where display badges beat printed badges

Printed badges are static and easy to miss. Display badges can rotate between sponsor identity, staff name, campaign graphic, and QR route. They also help sponsors reuse the same hardware across multiple events and partner programs.

Beambox fit

Beambox Nikko fits sponsor activations that need a larger full-color visual and readable QR code. Nano fits compact ambassador identity and lightweight partner events.

FAQ

Can sponsors use wearable display badges at events?

Yes. They can show sponsor identity, staff roles, QR offers, and campaign links.

What should a sponsor badge QR code link to?

A giveaway, trial, form, app download, product page, booking page, or event microsite.

Are display badges reusable across sponsor events?

Yes. That is one of their strongest advantages over printed sponsor badges.
