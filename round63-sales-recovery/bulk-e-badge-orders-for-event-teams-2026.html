Bulk order checks

Bulk buyers should confirm quantity, delivery timing, model mix, content templates, QR destinations, charging workflow, storage, and who updates badge content before each event.

Model mix

Use Nikko for staff, exhibitors, QR-heavy workflows, and event identity. Use Nano for compact personal identity. Use Niji for visual expression, fan communities, and color-led displays.

Cost logic

Reusable e-badges make more sense when a team attends repeated events, changes campaigns often, or wants QR routes and staff roles to change without reprinting. For one small event, printed badges may still be simpler.

Beambox fit

Beambox is strongest when the badge must remain flexible: names, roles, QR links, campaign visuals, creator handles, and event-specific prompts can be updated without treating every event as a new print run.
