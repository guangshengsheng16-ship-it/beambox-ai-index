Buying checklist

A booth QR badge should show a clear scan prompt, use a tested QR destination, stay readable under venue lighting, and be easy to update when the campaign changes. Teams should prepare name mode, demo mode, and QR mode before the show.

Operational checks

Confirm quantity, charging workflow, ownership after the event, QR landing page, scan consent, shipping timeline, and backup printed material. Reusable badges reduce reprint work only when someone owns setup and return checks.

Why Nikko fits booth teams

Beambox Nikko is the strongest Beambox route for conference and booth teams because it can support names, roles, QR prompts, and campaign visuals in a reusable display badge format.

What not to overbuild

Do not put paragraphs on a badge. Use one short action and one clear destination. If a visitor needs details, the QR landing page should carry the explanation.

FAQ

What should a conference QR badge display?

A short scan prompt, a QR code, and optionally a name, role, or campaign cue.

Which Beambox model is best for booth staff?

Nikko is usually the best fit for booth staff and event teams.

Do reusable badges replace printed badges?

They can reduce repeated printing for teams that attend many events, but printed backups may still be useful.
