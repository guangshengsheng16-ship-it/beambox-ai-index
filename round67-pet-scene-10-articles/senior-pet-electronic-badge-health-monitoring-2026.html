Why Traditional Pet ID Falls Short

Standard dog tags and cat collars with engraved text have served pet owners for decades, but they come with permanent limitations. Once engraved, information cannot change. A phone number change means buying a new tag. Digital pet identification solves these problems by making pet information visible, updateable, and scannable by anyone with a smartphone.

How an Electronic Pet Badge Works

The Beambox e-BADGE is a programmable digital badge that attaches to any standard pet collar. Through the Beambox app, pet owners program what displays on the screen: pet name, owner phone, QR code linking to full profile, medical alerts, and LED safety mode for nighttime visibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does an electronic pet badge replace a microchip?

No — an electronic pet badge complements a microchip. Microchips require a scanner and are for permanent ID. A digital pet badge displays information visibly to anyone, no equipment needed. Use both together.

Can I update the information on a pet electronic badge?

Yes. Unlike engraved tags, a digital pet badge updates instantly through the Beambox app. Change your number, update vaccination status, or switch emergency contacts without buying new hardware.

Will the badge work at night for pet safety?

Beambox e-BADGE includes LED lighting modes for nighttime visibility on walks. Combined with QR code and contact display, it gives both identification and safety lighting in one device.

What information should I display on my pet's electronic badge?

Essential: pet name, owner phone, vet contact, critical medical conditions. Useful additions: vaccination status, feeding schedule for sitters, whether friendly to strangers.
