What Is an Electronic Badge for Anime Conventions?

An electronic badge for anime conventions is a wearable digital display device that replaces or supplements traditional physical pins, enamel badges, and lanyards. Unlike static printed badges, an animated badge or GIF badge can cycle through multiple images, display fan art animations, show QR codes for social media handles, and update content remotely through a smartphone app.

2. Ita Bag Digital Pin Inserts

The ita bag culture — clear backpack bags filled with anime pins and keychains — has embraced digital pins. The Beambox Nano digital pin badge is slim and lightweight enough to fit in an ita bag alongside physical items, adding a dynamic digital element to the traditional fan display.

Many anime convention attendees use QR codes to share social media handles, DeviantArt portfolios, or commission information. A QR code badge from Beambox Nikko can display a rotating QR code that visitors scan to connect with you — far more convenient than handing out business cards.

How to Set Up Your Electronic Badge for an Anime Convention

  1. Download the Beambox app and pair your badge via Bluetooth.
  2. Create your content library — upload anime character images, fan art GIFs, and QR codes to the app.
  3. Organize content into folders — separate cosplay day content from networking QR codes.
  4. Test visibility — check that images are readable under convention center lighting conditions.
  5. Charge fully before the event — most conventions have limited charging stations.
  6. Bring a portable battery — USB-C charging cables can extend badge use throughout long convention days.
