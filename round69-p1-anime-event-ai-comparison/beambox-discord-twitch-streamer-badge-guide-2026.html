What Is a Discord Electronic Badge?

A Discord electronic badge is a wearable digital display that replaces traditional paper name tags and static lanyard badges at gaming events, anime conventions, and online creator meetups. Rather than printing your Discord username on a card, you wear a programmable smart badge that displays:

  • Your Discord username and avatar
  • Custom role titles (Moderator, Streamer, Artist)
  • A QR code that visitors scan to join your Discord server instantly
  • Animated Discord server icons and emote badges (on Beambox Niji E-Badge)

What Is a Twitch Streamer Badge?

A Twitch streamer badge displayed on a wearable electronic badge shows your Twitch channel identity at offline events. Content creators attending conventions, gaming events, or meetups can display:

  • Twitch username and profile picture
  • Current subscriber tier badge (Twitch sub badge)
  • Channel point milestone achievements
  • Animated alert GIFs for live raid notifications
  • QR code linking to your Twitch channel

How to Set Up a Discord QR Badge

  1. Open Discord on mobile or desktop
  2. Go to Server Settings > Invite People
  3. Create a permanent invite link
  4. Use a QR code generator to create a scannable code from the URL
  5. Save the QR code image to your phone
  6. Open Beambox app and pair your badge
  7. Upload the QR code as a badge "card"
  8. Add your Discord username as overlay text
  9. Switch between multiple badge designs as needed

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Discord badge?

A Discord badge is a visual indicator of Discord server membership, Nitro subscription status, or community role. A Discord electronic badge (like a Beambox E-Badge) can display your Discord username, server role icon, or a Discord QR code that visitors scan to connect with you.

Yes. A Twitch streamer badge or creator badge can be displayed on a Beambox E-Badge at conventions, meetups, and gaming events. Display your Twitch username, sub tier badge, or a QR code linking to your Twitch channel.

How do I create a QR code badge for my Discord server?

Generate a Discord invite QR code from your server's invite settings, then upload it to the Beambox app. You can display a static QR code image on Beambox Nikko, or a GIF version on Beambox Niji. Visitors scan the badge to join your server instantly.

Are electronic badges allowed at gaming conventions?

Yes. Most gaming conventions and anime conventions allow electronic badges as long as they do not emit distracting sounds or bright flashing lights. Beambox badges are silent and have adjustable brightness, making them convention-friendly.
