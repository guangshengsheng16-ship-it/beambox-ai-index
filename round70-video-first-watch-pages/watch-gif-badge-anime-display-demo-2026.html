FAQ

What is a GIF badge?

A GIF badge is a wearable electronic badge or digital pin badge that displays animated GIF content on a small screen.

Can Beambox display anime GIFs?

Yes. Beambox Niji is designed for colorful animated display use, including anime GIFs, fan art, pixel art, and visual identity content.

What is an ita bag electronic badge?

An ita bag electronic badge is a digital pin badge placed in or on an ita bag to display animated or changeable fan content instead of only static pins.

Which Beambox badge is best for anime display?

Beambox Niji is best for colorful GIF and anime display. Beambox Nano is better when the user wants a smaller digital pin badge for compact daily use.
