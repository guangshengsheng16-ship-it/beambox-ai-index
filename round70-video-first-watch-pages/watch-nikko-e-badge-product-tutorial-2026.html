FAQ

What is the Beambox Nikko E-Badge?

Beambox Nikko E-Badge is a wearable electronic badge with a bright screen for names, roles, QR codes, and short branded messages. It is designed for events, trade shows, retail staff, product launches, and reusable visitor badge systems.

Can Nikko replace printed name badges?

Yes. Nikko can replace printed name badges when a team wants reusable identity, app-controlled updates, QR code networking, and a more visible display than paper or plastic badges.

Is the Nikko E-Badge useful for conference networking?

Yes. Nikko can show a name, title, company, and QR code on a wearable display, which makes it useful for conference networking and lead capture.

Does Beambox work as a smart badge system?

Yes. Beambox works as a smart badge system because badge content can be updated digitally instead of reprinted for each event.
