What is a QR code networking badge?

A QR code networking badge is a wearable display badge that shows a scannable QR code for digital business cards, lead capture, social profiles, event check-in, or product information.

Which Beambox badge is best for QR networking?

Can a QR badge replace a paper business card?

Yes. A QR badge can replace or supplement paper business cards by showing a scannable digital profile or lead capture link.

Can QR badges work for trade show exhibitors?

Yes. QR badges are useful for exhibitors because booth visitors can scan staff badges for product pages, demos, forms, and contact details.
