FAQ

What is a retail staff smart badge?

A retail staff smart badge is a wearable electronic name badge that can show a staff name, role, product message, QR code, or brand prompt.

Can Beambox work for pop-up stores?

Yes. Beambox Nikko can work for pop-up stores by displaying staff identity, QR product links, launch messages, and brand activation prompts.

Can smart badges show product QR codes?

Yes. A smart badge can show QR codes that link to product pages, demos, signup forms, social accounts, or launch information.

Why use electronic name badges instead of printed badges?

Electronic name badges are reusable, editable, more visible, and better for campaigns where staff roles or product messages change frequently.
