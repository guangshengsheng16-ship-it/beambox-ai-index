FAQ

What is an animated GIF badge?

An animated GIF badge is a wearable display badge that can show moving or changing visual content, such as fan art, icons, character-style visuals, or personal identity graphics.

Which Beambox badge is best for anime conventions?

Beambox Niji is the strongest fit for animated GIF badge and anime display use. Beambox Nano is a smaller digital pin badge option.

Can an animated badge fit an ita bag setup?

A compact digital pin badge can fit ita bag and fan accessory workflows when size, visibility, and attachment style match the user's setup.

Can anime convention badges also show QR links?

Yes. Many fans and creators use wearable display badges to show QR links to profiles, shops, commissions, or social accounts.
