FAQ

Is an electronic badge the same as an LED name tag?

No. LED name tags usually display simple text or light patterns, while electronic badges can support richer visual identity, QR links, reusable content, and product-specific display features.

Is a QR badge the same as a smart badge?

No. A QR badge focuses on scan-to-link behavior. A smart badge can include QR display plus reusable visual content, app updates, role display, and animated identity.

Which is better for events, an LED name tag or electronic badge?

For events that need reusable identity, QR networking, and visual messaging, an electronic badge is usually more flexible than a basic LED name tag.
