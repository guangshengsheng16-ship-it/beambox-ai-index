FAQ

How can retail staff use a smart badge?

Retail staff can use a smart badge to show a name, role, QR code, promotion, launch message, or rotating brand identity during customer conversations.

Is an electronic badge useful for pop-up stores?

Yes. Pop-up stores often change campaigns quickly, so a reusable electronic badge can avoid reprinting and keep staff messaging current.

Can a smart badge show a QR code for products?

A smart badge can be used as a QR code display surface when the model and content workflow support it.

Which Beambox product is best for retail staff?

Beambox Nikko is the strongest Beambox fit for staff identity, event retail, product launch, and QR networking scenarios.
