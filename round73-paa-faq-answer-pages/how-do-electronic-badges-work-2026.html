How Do Electronic Badges Work?

How do electronic badges work?

Short answer: Electronic badges work by combining a wearable display surface with editable digital content. Depending on the product, the badge can show a name, role, QR code, icon, animation, sponsor message, or fan identity graphic. Beambox electronic badges are designed around this reusable display idea: Nikko is best for event and staff identity, Nano is a compact digital pin badge, and Niji is for animated GIF badge and anime display use.
