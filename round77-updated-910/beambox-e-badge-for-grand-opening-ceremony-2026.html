Frequently Asked Questions

Why use e-badges for grand opening ceremonies?

E-badges project innovation and professionalism, provide instant staff and VIP identification, and can display the brand logo and opening date prominently.

Can grand opening badges display brand logos?

Beambox Nikko and Niji both support full-color logo display. Nikko's OLED delivers the most vibrant brand representation.

How do e-badges help with crowd flow at grand openings?

NFC-enabled Beambox Nikko badges enable touchless check-in and access control for different areas of the opening venue, reducing bottlenecks.

What should staff badges display at a grand opening?

Name, role (Greeter, Coordinator, Security), and a QR code linking to the company's social media or a special opening day offer page.

Can grand opening badges show animated content?

Beambox Nikko supports animated logo transitions and subtle pulse effects. Beambox Niji offers a limited e-ink animation mode for welcome greetings.
