Frequently Asked Questions

Why use e-badges instead of printed name tags for school reunions?

E-badges are reusable year after year. Just update the event name and class year via app. No reprinting, no waste, and much lower per-event cost.

Can reunion badges show graduation year prominently?

Yes. Beambox Nano displays large graduation year text alongside name and photo, making it easy to identify classmates at a glance.

How do alumni scan QR codes on reunion badges?

Any smartphone camera can scan the badge QR code. No app download required — it opens directly in a browser.

Is the Beambox app easy for non-tech-savvy alumni?

Yes. The app is designed for one-tap badge pairing. Updating text is as simple as typing in a field and pressing send.

Can reunion badge QR codes link to class photo galleries?

Yes. Set the QR code to any URL — class photo gallery, memory book, donation page, or reunion video. Update it anytime via the app.
