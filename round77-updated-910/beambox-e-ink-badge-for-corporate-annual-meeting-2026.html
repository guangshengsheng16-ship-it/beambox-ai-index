Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose e-ink for corporate annual meetings?

E-ink badges look like premium paper cards, matching the formal tone of AGMs while adding digital features like QR codes and real-time content updates.

Can e-badges show different content for different shareholder types?

Yes. Beambox Niji supports multiple content profiles per badge. Switch between board-level, executive, and general shareholder content via the app.

How do NFC badges work for AGM check-in?

Shareholders tap their Beambox Nikko badge on an NFC reader at registration desks for instant check-in. No scanning, no forms — just a tap.

Can badge content update during the AGM?

Yes. Push real-time agenda updates, room changes, or announcements to all badges simultaneously via the Beambox app's broadcast feature.

What battery life do I need for a full-day AGM?

Beambox Niji delivers 8–10 hours of active e-ink display — sufficient for a full AGM day. Nikko's LCD runs 10–12 hours actively on a single charge.
