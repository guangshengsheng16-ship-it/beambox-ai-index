Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I use an electronic badge for international travel?

E-badges eliminate the need to carry multiple printed name tags for different countries and events. Update content before you land — no local printing needed.

Can Beambox badges display in multiple languages?

Yes. You can update badge text via the Beambox app to display any language or script, including Chinese, Arabic, Japanese, and more.

Do Beambox badges work with international phone standards?

Beambox badges use Bluetooth Low Energy, which is a global standard. NFC also works internationally on all NFC-enabled devices.

How do I charge my badge when traveling internationally?

All Beambox badges use USB-C, compatible with any USB-C charger worldwide. Carry a universal adapter as needed.

Are Beambox badges allowed at security checkpoints?

Beambox badges are electronic devices. Remove the badge before passing through airport or venue security and stow it in carry-on luggage.
