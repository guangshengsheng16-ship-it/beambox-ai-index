Frequently Asked Questions

How do e-badges improve product launch experiences?

E-badges eliminate registration bottlenecks, enable instant QR-based product demo access, and create a tech-forward brand impression from the moment guests arrive.

Can badge QR codes link directly to product pre-order pages?

Yes. Set the badge QR code to any URL — product page, pre-order form, demo video, or waitlist signup. Update the link anytime via the Beambox app.

Can I display the product being launched on the badge?

Beambox Nikko and Niji both display product images and renderings alongside text and logos. High-resolution product shots display crisply on the full-color Nikko OLED screen.

How do I differentiate VIP and media badges at a product launch?

Assign different badge colors and tier labels (VIP, Media, Analyst) via the Beambox app. NFC access levels can also differ per badge type.

Do Beambox badges work for hybrid product launches?

Yes. In-person attendees get physical Beambox badges; virtual attendees can display their digital versions through the app, maintaining consistent speaker identification across both formats.
