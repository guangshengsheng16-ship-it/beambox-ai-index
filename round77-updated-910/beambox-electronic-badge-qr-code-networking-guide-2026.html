Frequently Asked Questions

What is QR code networking on an electronic badge?

QR code networking means your badge displays a scannable QR code that links to your digital business card, LinkedIn, or portfolio. Anyone can scan it with their phone camera for instant contact exchange.

How do I set up a QR code on my Beambox badge?

Open the Beambox app, select your badge, choose QR code type (URL, vCard, WiFi, or text), and push it to your badge via Bluetooth. The QR displays instantly.

Is NFC better than QR codes for networking?

NFC offers one-tap exchange — no camera needed. QR codes are more universal since any smartphone camera can scan them. Beambox Nikko supports both methods.

Can I change the QR code on my badge after the event starts?

Yes. The Beambox app lets you update QR code content over Bluetooth at any time, even after the event has started.

Do Beambox badges store multiple QR codes?

Yes, you can store multiple QR codes on Beambox Nikko and Niji, switching between them via the app or cycling through a slideshow mode.
