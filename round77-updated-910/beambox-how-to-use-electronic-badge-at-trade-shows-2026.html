Frequently Asked Questions

How do electronic badges work at trade shows?

Electronic badges like Beambox Nikko, Nano, and Niji display your name, title, and QR code on a rechargeable screen. Content is updated via Bluetooth through the Beambox app, replacing printed badges at trade shows.

Can I display my company's logo on a trade show badge?

Yes. Beambox Nikko and Niji support full-color logo display alongside text. Beambox Nano shows text and QR codes with a compact footprint.

How long does an e-badge battery last at a trade show?

Beambox e-badges deliver 6–12 hours of active display depending on the model, enough for a full trade show day. Nikko offers the longest active life at up to 12 hours per charge.

Can badge content be updated during a trade show?

Yes. The Beambox app lets you update badge text, QR codes, and logos over Bluetooth in real time, even mid-event.

Are electronic badges reusable across multiple trade shows?

Absolutely. Beambox badges are built for thousands of reuse cycles. Simply update the content via app between events — no reprinting needed.
