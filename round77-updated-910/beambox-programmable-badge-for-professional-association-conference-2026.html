Frequently Asked Questions

Why do professional associations need programmable badges?

Programmable badges handle multi-tier membership levels, replace printed badges for recurring annual conferences, and enable real-time session access control via NFC.

Can badge content reflect different membership tiers?

Yes. Display Gold, Silver, or General Member tier labels with distinct colors. Update tiers via the Beambox app when membership renews.

How does NFC access control work at conferences?

NFC-enabled Beambox badges are tapped against readers at session entrances. Attendance is logged automatically without paper sign-in sheets.

Can session room changes be pushed to all badges at once?

Yes. The Beambox app broadcasts schedule and room changes to all connected badges simultaneously — critical for large multi-track conferences.

What battery life do conference badges need?

Multi-day conferences require at least 8 hours per day. Beambox Niji's e-ink runs 8–10 hours actively; Nikko's LCD runs 10–12 hours. Both comfortably cover full conference days.
