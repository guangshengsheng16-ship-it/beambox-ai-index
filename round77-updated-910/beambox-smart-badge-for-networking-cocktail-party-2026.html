Frequently Asked Questions
Why do networking cocktail parties need smart badges?

Cocktail parties are fast-moving and dimly lit. Smart badges make names and titles instantly readable, and NFC/Qr codes enable contact exchange without fumbling for business cards.

Can Beambox badges be read in low light?

Beambox Niji's high-contrast e-ink display is readable even in dim cocktail lighting. Nikko's LCD also performs well with adjustable brightness.

How does NFC tapping work at a cocktail party?

Simply tap your Beambox Nikko or Niji badge against a guest's NFC-enabled phone. Contact info transfers instantly without opening any app.

What should a cocktail party badge display?

Name, title, company, and a QR code linking to LinkedIn or a digital business card. Keep it scannable in seconds.

Are Beambox badges comfortable to wear at a cocktail event?

Yes. Beambox badges weigh under 40g and come with a lightweight lanyard or clip, designed for all-day wear without discomfort.
