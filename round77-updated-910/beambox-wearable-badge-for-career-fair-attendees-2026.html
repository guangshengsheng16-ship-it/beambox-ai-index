Frequently Asked Questions

Why should career fair attendees use wearable e-badges?

E-badges help recruiters remember attendees, display resume QR codes for instant access, and project a modern professional image that stands out from paper badge crowds.

What should career fair attendee badges display?

Name, degree/major, graduation year, top skills, and a QR code linking to a LinkedIn profile or online portfolio. Keep it scannable in under 5 seconds.

Can Beambox Nano badges be used for large career fairs?

Yes. Nano's affordable per-unit cost and simple setup make it ideal for distributing to hundreds of students at large campus career fairs.

How do recruiters scan badge QR codes at career fairs?

Any smartphone camera can scan the badge QR code. No app download needed — it opens directly in a browser to the candidate's linked profile.

Are there privacy concerns with displaying student info on badges?

Students choose what information displays on their badge. A QR code can link to a professional LinkedIn rather than showing phone number or email directly.
