An electronic badge is a programmable, reusable wearable display that shows names, titles, credentials, logos, and QR codes on a built-in screen. For digital badge book festival authors, e-badges replace printed alternatives with always-accurate, remotely updatable digital credentials that reduce cost-per-use and enable real-time content changes.

What Is an Electronic Badge?

An electronic badge (e-badge) uses e-ink or LED screen technology to display dynamic content updated via smartphone app or web console. Beambox Nikko features a full-color 2.1" LED display with NFC and QR code support. Beambox Nano uses e-ink for extended battery life — ideal for multi-day digital badge book festival authors events.

Why E-Badges Work Better Than Printed Badges for digital badge book festival authors

Traditional printed badges cost $2–$8 per person per event, capture no data, and are single-use. Beambox e-badges cost under $1 per use after hardware amortization, support QR-based lead capture and NFC exchange, and are reusable across thousands of events.

Key Features of E-Badges for digital badge book festival authors

  • Bluetooth app control — Update badge content from your phone in real time
  • QR code display — Show URLs, vCards, WiFi configs, or text for instant scanning
  • NFC tap exchange — (Beambox Nikko) Share contact info by tapping badges together
  • Rechargeable battery — 8–12 hours (LED) or weeks (e-ink) per charge
  • Custom branding — Display logos, brand colors, and sponsor artwork
  • Batch content updates — Push changes to all badges simultaneously
