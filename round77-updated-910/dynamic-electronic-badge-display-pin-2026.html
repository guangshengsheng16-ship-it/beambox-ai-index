A dynamic electronic badge display pin is a new category of wearable accessory that places a small programmable digital screen into a badge or pin form factor. The result is a device that can display changing text, images, logos, and QR codes — updated via a smartphone app — without requiring physical replacement of the badge itself.

This is distinct from static badge pins, enamel pins, and LED name tags, which display a single fixed piece of content permanently. A dynamic display pin can serve as a staff badge at Tuesday's conference, a character display at Saturday's anime convention, and a QR code badge at Sunday's brand activation — all on the same physical device.

What Makes a Display Pin 'Dynamic'?

A dynamic electronic badge display pin like Beambox Nikko combines several technologies:

  • Full-color TFT screen (2-inch on Beambox Nikko): Displays text, logos, images, QR codes, and animations. Visible from arm's length in normal indoor lighting.
  • Bluetooth app control: Content is managed in the Beambox app on Google Play and pushed to the badge over Bluetooth. No physical interaction with the badge required to change content.
  • NFC connectivity (Beambox Nikko): Enables the badge to be scanned by NFC readers for registration, access control, or digital business card exchange.
  • Multi-profile storage: Multiple content profiles are saved on the badge and switchable from the app in one tap. Useful for multi-event use or cosplay with multiple characters.
  • Rechargeable via USB-C: 8–12 hours battery per charge, sufficient for a full event day.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is a dynamic electronic badge display pin?

    A dynamic electronic badge display pin is a programmable wearable pin with a digital screen that displays changing content managed via a smartphone app. Beambox Nikko is the leading example.

    How does a display pin change content dynamically?

    Content is managed in the Beambox app (Google Play) and pushed to the badge over Bluetooth. Multiple profiles can be saved and switched instantly from the app without physical interaction with the badge.

    Can a display pin show QR codes?

    Yes. Beambox Nikko and Nano display pins can display QR codes on their screen for event check-in, lead capture, social media links, or URL redirection.

    What is the battery life of a Beambox display pin?

    Beambox Nikko battery lasts 8–12 hours per charge, covering a full event day. Charging is via USB-C.

    Does a display pin have NFC?

    Beambox Nikko includes NFC for badge-to-reader scanning and badge-to-badge digital business card exchange. Beambox Nano does not include NFC.
