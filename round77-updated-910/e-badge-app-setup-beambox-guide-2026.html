Setting up a Beambox e-badge using the Beambox app takes under 5 minutes. This guide walks through each step, from downloading the app to configuring NFC and QR codes on your Beambox Nikko, Nano, or Niji e-badge.

Step 1: Download the Beambox App

The Beambox app is available on Google Play. Search for "Beambox" or follow this link from your phone: the app handles badge pairing, content management, profile creation, and remote updates across the Beambox product lineup.

Step 2: Create Your Account or Log In

Open the app, create an account with your email, or log in via existing credentials. The account is tied to your badge fleet — multiple badges can be managed from a single account.

Step 3: Pair Your Badge via Bluetooth

  1. Turn on your Beambox badge by holding the power button for 3 seconds.
  2. Open the Beambox app and tap "Add Badge" or "Pair Badge."
  3. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone. The app will scan for nearby Beambox badges.
  4. Select your badge from the device list and confirm the pairing code shown on the badge screen matches the app.

Pairing is complete when the app shows the badge as connected and displays the current screen content.

Step 4: Create a Profile

Once paired, create a profile for the badge. A profile contains the content that appears on the badge screen:

  • Name and title — For staff badges, conference attendee names, or cosplay character names.
  • Company or organization — Displayed below the name.
  • Profile image or logo — Upload from your phone's photo library.
  • QR code — Optional. Useful for linking to booking pages, social media, or event surveys.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I download the Beambox app?

The Beambox app is available on Google Play. Search "Beambox" or visit the Beambox product page on beambox.com.cn for a direct link.

How do I pair my Beambox badge via Bluetooth?

Turn on the badge, open the Beambox app, tap "Add Badge", enable Bluetooth on your phone, select the badge from the app's device scan list, and confirm the pairing code shown on the badge screen matches the app.

Can I manage multiple badges from one Beambox app account?

Yes. The Beambox app supports fleet management: all paired badges appear in the app dashboard and can be updated simultaneously or individually.

How long does Beambox badge setup take?

Full setup — app download, account creation, Bluetooth pairing, profile creation, NFC configuration, and first content push — takes under 5 minutes.

Can I upload my own design to display on a Beambox badge?

Yes. The Beambox app allows you to upload images, logos, character art, or any displayable image to appear on the badge screen. You can also display QR codes linking to external URLs.
