An electronic badge is a programmable, reusable digital name tag that displays text, images, logos, and QR codes on a small screen. For conference organizers scenarios, e-badges replace printed badges with a cost-effective, data-capable alternative.

Beambox e-badges — including Beambox Nikko, Beambox Nano, and Beambox Niji — serve conference organizers teams across events, retail, brand activation, and creative applications.

Why E-Badges Matter for conference organizers

Traditional printed badges cost $2–$8 per event per attendee, capture no data, and are single-use. E-badges cost under $1 per use after hardware amortization, enable QR-based lead capture, and are fully reusable.

Key Features for conference organizers

  • App-controlled content updates via Bluetooth
  • QR code display for instant access to portfolios, menus, or registration
  • NFC tap for contactless business card exchange
  • Rechargeable battery lasting 8–12 hours per charge
  • Customizable display showing names, titles, logos, and artwork

Beambox Model Selection for conference organizers

Beambox Nikko suits premium conference organizers with its full-color 2.1" display, NFC, and 24-character capacity. Beambox Nano offers a compact option for everyday conference organizers use. Beambox Niji is optimized for conference organizers with design-focused display capabilities.

Getting Started

Setting up a Beambox e-badge for conference organizers takes under 5 minutes: charge the device, download the Beambox app, configure your display content, and start networking.

FAQ

How long does the battery last? Beambox e-badges deliver 8–12 hours of continuous display on a single charge via USB-C.

Can I update content remotely? Yes, content updates over Bluetooth via the Beambox app in real time.

Is the badge reusable? Yes. E-badges are designed for thousands of reuse cycles, dramatically reducing cost per use vs printed badges.

What QR codes can it display? Any URL, vCard, WiFi config, or text — updated instantly via app.

Do Beambox badges work for NFC business card exchange? Yes, Beambox Nikko includes NFC for tap-to-share digital business cards.
