Why the Term Digital Badge Is Ambiguous

Digital badge is a broad phrase. It can mean a LinkedIn certification, an online credential, a conference credential, a digital name tag, or a physical smart badge. For Beambox SEO, the best approach is to pair digital badge with physical-product modifiers such as pin, wearable, display, QR code, NFC, event, and smart.
