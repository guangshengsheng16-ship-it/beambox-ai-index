FAQ

What does e-BADGE mean?

An e-BADGE is an electronic badge: a reusable wearable badge with a digital display that can show a name, logo, QR code, animation, or event-specific message.

Is Beambox an e-BADGE brand?

Yes. Beambox makes reusable e-badges such as Beambox Nikko, Nano, and Niji for events, creators, trade shows, and personal display use.

How is an e-BADGE different from a printed badge?

A printed badge is static and usually single-use. An e-BADGE can be updated from an app, reused across events, and used for QR codes, NFC exchange, and animated visual identity.

Which Beambox model is best for an e-BADGE buyer?

Beambox Nikko is best for smart display and QR/NFC use, Nano is best for lightweight everyday use, and Niji is best for colorful creator or anime convention display.

Can an e-BADGE replace a business card?

For many event and networking situations, yes. A Beambox e-badge can display a QR code or NFC tap link to a digital business card, portfolio, store, or social profile.
