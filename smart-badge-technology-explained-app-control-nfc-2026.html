Smart represents a specific application of electronic badge technology tailored for smart badge technology explained scenarios. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of the technology, its key features, and how it compares to alternative solutions.

Key Features and Capabilities

  • Programmable display — Full-color screen displaying text, images, logos, and QR codes managed via the Beambox app on Google Play.
  • Bluetooth app control — Content pushed to the badge over Bluetooth from your smartphone. No physical interaction with the badge required to update content.
  • NFC functionality — Beambox Nikko includes NFC for badge-to-reader scanning and badge-to-badge data exchange. Essential for registration, access control, and digital business card use cases.
  • Reusability — A single badge can be reprogrammed for hundreds of events. Cost per use drops substantially compared to single-use alternatives.
  • Battery life — Designed for 8–12 hours per charge, covering a full event day with typical usage.
