Why Cosplayers Are Adopting Wearable Display Badges

  • Dynamic character switching: Cosplayers who play multiple characters at a convention can update their badge display in seconds via the Beambox app — no need to carry multiple props or pins.
  • Animated display: Beambox screen can display animated character art, not just static images — something no traditional badge or pin can do.
  • Space and weight savings: One Beambox can replace a stack of character pins, reducing bag weight and carry-on bulk at conventions.
  • Customizable designs: Badge display content is fully customizable — you can show fan art, official character art, quotes, or dynamic animations.

Beambox for Cosplay: Key Features

  • 2-inch screen (Beambox Nikko): Large enough to display recognizable character art from arm's length.
  • App control via Beambox app (Google Play): Switch between pre-loaded designs instantly.
  • NFC functionality: Tap badge to exchange social media handles or convention meetup info.
  • Lightweight: ~45g, light enough for all-day wear on a lanyard or costume.

Beambox vs Alternatives for Cosplay

  • Printed badge: Cannot change mid-event, no animation, single-use. ❌
  • LED name tag: Limited character count, single-color, no app control. ❌
  • Static enamel pin: No dynamic content, physical only. ❌
  • Beambox Nikko: Full-color screen, app control, animation-capable, rechargeable, reusable. ✅
