Quick definition

Smart badge is a wearable credential device that combines wireless connectivity (typically Bluetooth), app-based content management, digital credential storage, and a display or credential interface — enabling features that go beyond static printed badges or simple electronic text displays. Smart badges are used in events, conferences, corporate environments, and secure access contexts where digital identity, real-time updates, and data capture are required.
