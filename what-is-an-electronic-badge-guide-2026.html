What Is an Electronic Badge? A Complete Guide for Events, Conferences & Trade Shows

Primary keyword: what is an electronic badge

An electronic badge (e-badge) is a rechargeable wearable display device that shows names, QR codes, and animations. Unlike printed badges, e-badges can be updated in real time via Bluetooth and a mobile app.
