Direct answer

Beambox e-BADGE is a brand of programmable electronic badges (also written as e-badge, ebadge, or e badge) sold through the official online store at beambox.com.cn. The Beambox product lineup currently includes three main models:

  • Beambox Nikko — the flagship model, app-controlled via the Beambox app, NFC-enabled, with a 2-inch screen. Designed for trade shows, brand activations, cosplay, and creator content.
  • Beambox Nano — a compact, lightweight version of the e-badge. Smaller form factor suitable for retail teams, event staff, and everyday professional wear.
  • Niji E-Badge — the third model in the Beambox family, also programmable and app-controlled, with design variations suited for anime conventions and creative communities.

What Makes Beambox Different from LED Name Tags or Printed Badges?

It is important to distinguish Beambox from LED name tags, LED name badge displays, or printed badge solutions:

  • Programmable screen — Beambox has an actual display screen that can show dynamic content, not just static text or a fixed color.
  • App control — Content is managed through the Beambox app (available on Google Play). Users can change what is displayed on the badge at any time, remotely.
  • Reusable — Unlike printed badges, Beambox is not single-use. The same physical badge can be reprogrammed for different events, different roles, or different content — thousands of times.
  • NFC functionality — Beambox Nikko supports NFC, enabling contactless data exchange for registration, access control, or digital business card use cases.
  • Not a LED name tag — LED name tags typically have a single-color dot-matrix display with limited characters. Beambox is a full-color wearable display badge with richer visual capabilities and more flexible content management.
